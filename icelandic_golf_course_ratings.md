## Notes on Sources

1. **grgolf.is** — Official site of Golfklúbbur Reykjavíkur. Provided downloadable vallarmat PDFs (2024-2025) with course rating and slope by teigur (tee marker number) for both men (karlar) and women (konur).

2. **oddur.is** — Official site of Golfklúbburinn Oddur. Provided a CertificationPoster PDF (Aug 2023) certified by Golfsamband Íslands (GSÍ) with full WHS course rating data including front/back nine breakdowns.

3. **golficeland.org** — Official platform of the Icelandic Golf and Tourism Association. Provided course rating tables for most clubs with tee colors, distances in meters, CR, and Slope. Some data may be older than club-specific sources.

4. **golfkeilir.is** and **golfkopavogur.is** — Both timed out during fetching. The correct URL for Kópavogur golf is gkg.is / gkggolf.is.

5. **ga.is** and **gks.is** — These are NOT golf clubs. ga.is = GA Smíðajárn (steel company), gks.is = GKS Innréttingar (furniture). Correct URLs: gagolf.is (Akureyri) and gs.is (Suðurnesja).

6. **Mosfellsbær (golfmos.is)** — CR/Slope data exists on the website but is embedded in images that could not be extracted as text. Only tee distances were available in text form.

7. **Teigur system** — Some Icelandic courses use a numbered "teigur" system (e.g., 46, 49, 52, 57, 63) instead of color-based tees. The number roughly corresponds to the tee position.
